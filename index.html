Sound Design

POLARA is a 16-voice polyphonic subtractive/FM synthesizer built around a custom Moog ladder filter — designed for tight, sharp filter movement in techno, trance, DnB, and modern electronic production.

  • 16-voice polyphony with FM + AM cross-modulation and Hard Sync between oscillators
  • 10 filter modes including Allpass and Comb — Karplus-Strong plucks straight from the filter
  • Custom FastLadderFilter — 100× faster cutoff response than standard JUCE ladder
  • Dual LFOs with 5 waveforms, tempo-synced 1/2 to 1/64 including Triplet subdivisions
  • Full FX chain: Distortion · Chorus · Reverse Delay · FDN8 Reverb (6 algorithms) · Saturator
